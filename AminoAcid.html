  • Entry 1: Feeling Bad for Bethe?
    6/18/2017

    I don't feel bad, but she is hated beyond what is necessary.

    She is obviously not the greatest of fighters, nor athletes, we all know that. She trash talks for the same reason that many others trash talk - to sell the fight. It shouldn't be a reason to hate, especially when you know that behind the scenes she's not that person, as evident by the sauna picture. This isn't Ronda, Bethe is actually a respectable individual out of the octagon.

    Her face has become a huge target of attack. Had it not been for her stunning body she most likely wouldn't recieve that heat.

    It doesn't really matter though, I mean, it's sherdog afterall.
